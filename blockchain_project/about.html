About GoFundGood

GoFundGood is a blockchain crowdfunding platform for public emergency.


1. NPOs initiate projects by providing specific plans in different phases, target amount and beneficiary.


2. Donors select prefered projects and donate certain amount of money into an Escrow account.


3. When the funding in escrow account reaches 60% of target amount, 30% of target amount would be transferred to NPOs account as Phase 1.


4. NPOs use donations as planned and contact suppliers to get materials.


5. Supplies shipped to the beneficiary.


6. Beneficiary checks quality of the delivery and provides feedbacks regarding to Phase 1.


7. If the beneficiary thinks the delivery is qualified, the funding process will continue to next phase.


8. As funding amount reaches 80% of the target amount, another 40% of target amount would be transferred to NPOs account as Phase 2.


9. The same transactions continue as funding processed to the final Phase.


10. During any phases, if the beneficiary doesn’t get the delivery or received disqualified suppliers,

the funding would be ceased.


11. Funding remaining in the escrow account would be returned to donors respectively.
